Services

At Watchtow3r Medical Centre - GP Practice, we offer a wide range of medical services tailored to meet the unique needs of our patients. Our team of experienced healthcare professionals is dedicated to providing personalized and comprehensive care to help you achieve optimal health.

Cardiovascular Health Services

Our cardiovascular health services are designed to help you maintain a healthy heart and manage any heart-related conditions you may have. From routine check-ups to advanced diagnostic testing and treatment options, our experienced team is here to provide you with the care and support you need.

Pharmacy Services

We understand the importance of timely access to medication, which is why we offer convenient and efficient pharmacy services onsite

Hospital Care Services

In the event you require hospital care, we provide a wide range of services to support you through your treatment and recovery journey.

Genetic Testing and Counseling Services

Our genetic testing and counseling services offer patients personalized insights into their genetic makeup and risk factors for certain conditions. Our certified genetic counselors work with you to understand your unique needs, and provide guidance and support in making informed decisions about your health.

Mobility and Rehabilitation Services

Our mobility and rehabilitation services are designed to help you regain independence and improve your quality of life.

Chronic Disease Management Services

For patients living with chronic conditions, our chronic disease management services provide comprehensive care and support to help you manage your condition and live life to the fullest.
